About Us

At Bengaluru Riders, we're more than just a cycling group we're a tight-knit community of passionate cyclists united by the love for pedal-powered adventures and the vibrant city of Bengaluru. Whether you're an experienced cyclist or just starting your journey, our group offers a supportive and inclusive space for riders of all levels.

Our mission is to promote a healthy and active lifestyle through cycling while fostering a sense of camaraderie among fellow riders. We aim to create an environment where individuals can come together, share their experiences, and inspire each other to achieve their cycling goals.

Types of Rides


  • Road Cycling

    With sleek bicycles and aerodynamic gear, road cycling showcases the perfect blend of human effort and technological innovation.


  • Off-Road Cycling

    Where the thrill of the trail meets the freedom of the wilderness, creating a two-wheeled journey like no other.


  • BRM/Long Distance Cycling

    Embraces the spirit of long-distance adventure, pushing cyclists to test their limits and explore the world's roads with endurance and camaraderie.
